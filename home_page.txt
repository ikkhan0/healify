 
 
 
 
 
 
About TeleMind 
TeleMind is a specialized digital platform providing Mental Health and Psychosocial Support 
(MHPSS) in an effective, confidential, and holistic manner. It is developed and run by the 
National Institute of Psychology (NIP), Centre of Excellence, Quaid-i-Azam University, a 
premier government academic and research institution (visit www.nip.edu.pk), with the support 
of Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) to facilitate sustainable 
MHPSS services across Pakistan in 2026.  
Who Can Get Benefit? 
• 
Anyone in need of professional psychological guidance in a least stigmatized way 
through maintaining their privacy and confidentiality. 
• 
Anyone who is facing mental health problems and feels that their functioning is affected 
in home, social, occupational, school, or other areas of functioning and what assistance to 
improve coping. 
• 
One who is interested in gaining information on common mental health issues, 
underlying reasons, and learning self-help strategies. 
• 
Returning and potential migrants who want to seek mental MHPSS services in 
connection to relocation or reintegration challenges. 
• 
Women, children, the elderly, and minorities living in socio-economically deprived 
conditions. 
• 
People living in distant areas and where mental health services are not available in their 
close vicinity. 
What Will You Get? 
• 
Psychoeducation materials about common mental health problems and underlying 
reasons. 
• 
Self-assessment to timely pinpoint if you need any professional help.

• 
Self-help strategies to handle stressors and minor mental health issue. 
• 
Resources from where to get MHPSS and other services in different regions. 
• 
Professional counselling through our expert clinical psychologists, counsellors, speech 
therapists, remedial therapists, and career counsellors registered in TeleMind. 
• 
Referral through strategic links to healthcare providers and NGOs for specialized care, if 
needed. 
Who are MHPSS service providers? 
Mental health experts of NIP who have been providing in-person and online mental health 
services through NIP Counselling Centre are associated with TeleMind. If you need counselling 
and mental health services, as per your need and their expertise you can choose your mental 
health care provider including: 
•    Clinical psychologists,  
•    Counsellors,  
•    Speech therapists,  
•    Remedial therapists, 
•    Career Counsellors 
 
Credibility and Sustainability 
• 
It is made and run by a public sector institution where counselling rates are affordable. 
• 
In collaboration with other mental healthcare providing institutions and consultants it has 
network for referral. 
• 
Reliable and valid assessment and screening of mental health issues is accessible.
