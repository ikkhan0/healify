About TeleMind

TeleMind is a specialized digital platform providing Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) in an effective, confidential, and holistic manner. It is developed and run by the National Institute of Psychology (NIP), Centre of Excellence, Quaid-i-Azam University, a premier government academic and research institution, with the support of Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) to facilitate sustainable MHPSS services across Pakistan.

Who Can Get Benefit?

We provide a safe, stigmatized-free space for professional guidance.

Confidential Guidance

Anyone in need of professional psychological guidance in a least stigmatized way through maintaining privacy and confidentiality.

Daily Functioning Support

Anyone facing mental health problems whose functioning is affected in home, social, occupational, or school areas and wants assistance.

Information & Self-help

One who is interested in gaining information on common mental health issues, underlying reasons, and learning self-help strategies.

Migrants & Reintegration

Returning and potential migrants seeking MHPSS services in connection to relocation or reintegration challenges.

Vulnerable Groups

Women, children, the elderly, and minorities living in socio-economically deprived conditions.

Distant Communities

People living in distant areas where mental health services are not available in their close vicinity.

Credibility and Sustainability

Building a trustworthy foundation for mental healthcare.

  • It is made and run by a public sector institution where counselling rates are affordable.
  • In collaboration with other mental healthcare providing institutions and consultants, it has a network for referral.
  • Reliable and valid assessment and screening of mental health issues is accessible.
