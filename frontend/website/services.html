Our Services

TeleMind provides a comprehensive range of mental health and psychosocial support (MHPSS) services delivered by qualified professionals from the National Institute of Psychology.

Professional Counselling

One-on-one sessions with licensed clinical psychologists and counsellors via secure video conferencing.

  • Individual therapy sessions
  • Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT)
  • Trauma-focused counselling
  • Crisis intervention support

Psychological Assessments

Comprehensive mental health screening and assessments using validated clinical tools.

  • Generic self-assessment (PHQ-9)
  • Doctor-recommended specific assessments
  • Progress tracking over time
  • Confidential results with counsellor

Psychoeducation

Learn about common mental health conditions, their causes, and evidence-based coping strategies.

  • Depression & anxiety awareness
  • Trauma & PTSD education
  • Substance use information
  • Suicide prevention resources

Self-Help Strategies

Guided techniques you can practice independently to manage stress, anxiety, and emotional well-being.

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Mindfulness & guided imagery
  • Grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1)

Speech & Remedial Therapy

Specialized therapy services for communication disorders and learning difficulties.

  • Speech & language assessment
  • Articulation therapy
  • Learning disability support
  • Remedial education planning

Career Counselling

Professional guidance for career planning, academic choices, and vocational development.

  • Career aptitude assessment
  • Academic planning sessions
  • Workplace stress management
  • Professional development guidance
