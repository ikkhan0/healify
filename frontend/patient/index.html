Disclaimer & Privacy

Safety Disclaimer

This mobile application does not provide emergency services. If you or someone else are in immediate danger, please contact your nearest hospital emergency department, rescue services, embassy or a mental health helpline. Thank You.

Privacy Notice

Your information and identity are kept confidential. Names and personal details entered in this app/website will not appear on web searches or public platforms.

Psychoeducation

The information provided on this application is only for educational purposes and cannot be used for diagnosis purposes.

Screening

Assessment tools used for general information and not to be used for self-diagnosis purpose. It is meant for our counsellors to evaluate nature of problem and plan customized care for the client accordingly during consultation.
